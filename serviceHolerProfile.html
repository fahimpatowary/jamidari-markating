Each of our designers has a burning passion to transform houses into beautiful homes, no matter the budget.

You don’t have to invest epic amounts of time or money to make significant changes to your home. The secret is to identify the areas that will have the biggest impact with a bold upgrade.

These small yet distinctive updates will not only elevate your property’s resale potential and attract prospective buyers, they will also improve your quality of life, ensuring you and your family enjoy your home even more. Here, we ask interior design experts My Bespoke Room to pinpoint four simple yet impactful upgrades that will have buyers queuing at the door.

Bonus tip: Treat the ceiling as the fifth wall and decorate it with an eye-catching colour to bring attention to architectural features or make the walls feel taller. This decorating technique can also help define separate areas in an open-plan room, such as only painting the ceiling area above the dining or living area.
